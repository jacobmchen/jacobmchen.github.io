I graduated from Williams College in December 2023, receiving a B.A. with Honors in Computer Science and a concentration/minor in French and Francophone studies. My research interests are in developing statistical and machine learning methods for causal inference: the study of deducing causal quantities from observational data. I am also interested in missing data, measurement error, causal discovery, reliable decision-making, and applications in healthcare and the social sciences.

I wrote my undergraduate thesis with Professor Rohit Bhattacharya on recovering causal effects under the possibility of confounding bias and self-censoring missing data. My thesis may be accessed from the Williams College library archives here.
