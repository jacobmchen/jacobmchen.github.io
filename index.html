I am an undergraduate student at Williams College graduating in December 2023 with a B.A. in computer science and a concentration/minor in French and Francophone studies. My research interests are in developing statistical and machine learning methods for causal inference: the study of deducing causal quantities from observational data.

I wrote my undergraduate thesis with Professor Rohit Bhattacharya on recovering causal effects under the possibility of confounding bias and self-censoring missing data.
